Hi, I'm Natasha A. Elliott

I am interested in the brain-body connection and how it relates to mood disorders.

As a PhD researcher at the University of Nottingham, I am investigating the interaction between neural and visceral oscillations during therapeutic interventions and how this influences our ability to control thoughts and actions.

Prior to my current work, I studied at Cardiff and Keele University whilst assisting part-time in hospitals.

Outside of research I enjoy vegan cooking, gaming, reading, photography, running, yoga, learning Japanese and volunteering!
