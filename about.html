For my PhD research, I am investigating the interaction between neural and visceral oscillations during therapeutic interventions and how this influences our ability to control thoughts and actions.

Prior to my current work, I studied for an MSc in Neuroimaging: Methods and Applications, and even more prior I completed an undergraduate degree in Psychology. Alongside these degrees I worked in various hospitals and volunteered for mental health services.

Outside of research I enjoy vegan cooking, gaming, reading, photography, running, yoga and learning Japanese!
